How it works

Own stock, own invoice.

Stock

Own inventory in Lisbon for fast mobilisation in Lisbon and around. Beyond that, depends on window and quantity.

Inclui

Delivery, pickup, basic setup where applicable, batteries and consumables. Faulty units replaced at no cost.

Tarifas

On request, with tiers by unit count and number of days. Bundled rates when renting across categories.

Invoice & insurance

Invoice issued by Gentle Laughter. Equipment covered by operating insurance, with declared value on request.

How to book

How many units, which days, and where?

Send the category (one or several), unit count, days and location. We return a written quote with rate, transport, setup and applicable insurance.

Frequently asked

How much is sound and light rental for a wedding in Lisbon?

Varies with guest count, venue (indoor or outdoor) and duration. Base packages start from the minimum recommended setup for ceremony plus reception. Includes delivery, setup, supervision and pickup.

Do you have radios for event staff?

Yes. Rental of professional walkie-talkies — Motorola DP1400 and similar — with earpiece. Bundles from 4 units. Channel configuration by team.
