Event production, Lisbon.

From booking the venue to the last chair packed. Christenings, weddings, private events, festivals and arenas, led by a team that also produces, books and lights.

Tipologia

  • Batizados
  • Weddings
  • Private events
  • Large scale (festivals & arenas)

What's included

Prior coordination with couple, sponsors, hosts or promoters. Venue search and booking, catering, entertainment and suppliers. Technical schedule, rehearsal where applicable, day-of supervision, build and tear-down.

Large-scale events

For festivals, arenas and large-scale productions, Rock in Rio, MEO Arena, Coliseu, Campo Pequeno, Multiusos de Odivelas, we take on operational coordination, stagehands, drivers, security partner, equipment rental and on-the-ground crew management. Working in consortium with promoters and producers.

How we work

An initial meeting to understand scale, budget and non-negotiables. From there, a single point of contact bridges every supplier and the client decides only what they actually need to decide.

Orçamento

On request, with written proposal after the initial meeting.

Escala

From intimacy to the bigger stage.

From a ceremony in an intimate room to a festival stage, we keep a single interlocutor for the client.

Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost to organise a wedding in Lisbon?

Varies with scale, venue and type. Initial meeting at no cost. Written proposal after briefing, with itemised suppliers (catering, entertainment, set design, logistics).

Which areas do you cover for events?

Based in Lisbon, priority coverage in the metropolitan area, Sintra, Cascais, Oeiras, Almada, Setúbal. Available for travel to the rest of mainland Portugal on consultation of logistics.
