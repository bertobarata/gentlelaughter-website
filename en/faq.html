Rigour in the detail, clarity in the answer.

Find here the answers about how we operate. If you prefer a direct contact, we reply within hours.

What's your availability for new projects?

On request. Single point of contact from first inquiry to load-out. Structured proposals usually within the same day.

Do you work outside Greater Lisbon?

Yes, we cover the whole country. For projects requiring extended travel, we coordinate all crew accommodation and transfer logistics, itemised in the quote.

How is invoicing handled?

We issue tax invoices for all services, ensuring full fiscal transparency for both private clients and businesses (B2B).

Do you work with partner production agencies?

Yes. A large share of our work supports other production companies and agencies with operational crews (stagehands), special effects and technical logistics.

Do you issue invoices?

Always. We issue invoices to end clients and to businesses, with or without withholding tax as applicable.
