01 · Stagehands & loaders

Mãos no palco e no backstage.

Assistentes de palco para descarga, montagem de equipamento e cenários, operação em backstage e desmontagem. Equipas dimensionadas à escala da produção, pontuais, fardadas, com experiência em palco, festival e tour. Ferramenta básica incluída.

  • Build-up & load-in/out
  • Backstage operation and crew rotation
  • Estrados, truss, rigging e Set carpentry

How to book

One request, one quote, one roster.

Send date, location, event type and the number of people needed per role. We return a written quote with roster, uniforms, internal transport and associated logistics.

Frequently asked

How much does it cost to book stagehands for a concert in Lisbon?

Rate per person per hour, with a 4–6 hour minimum depending on the operation. Crew sized to event scale, in uniform, with basic tools included. Quote follows technical briefing.

Do you have TVDE-licensed drivers for band transfers?

Yes. In-house fleet of three vehicles (Mercedes V250, E-Class, light vehicle) with experienced drivers. TVDE licence available when required by the promoter or the rider.
