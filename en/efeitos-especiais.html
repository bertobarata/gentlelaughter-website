Special effects.

Pirotecnia fria, jactos de CO₂, fumos pesados e leves, hazers, canhões de confetti e streamers. Operação certificada, sincronizada com música ou cue de régie, do casamento ao mega-evento.

Catálogo

Six effect families.

Combinations designed around the cue sheet: which moment, which duration, which height, which stage framing.

01

Cold pyrotechnics

Stage sparklers and cold fountains for entries, exits and key moments. Compatible with low ceilings and indoor venues.

02

CO₂ jets

High-impact white columns for musical drops. 2 to 8-unit setups, synced from FOH.

03

Heavy fog

Floor-hugging white blanket for theatrical entries, first dance and photography. Dry-ice or low-fog machine.

04

Light fog & hazers

Atmospheric ambience that supports the lighting work. Constant density throughout the show.

05

Confetti cannons

Pneumatic or electric, reloadable. Slow-fall confetti, metallic or custom.

06

Streamers

Manual or electric, range up to 25 m. Show closure, ceremony "yes" confirmation, tour opening.

Applications

Where they work, and when.

Effects are chosen for the moment they serve, not the name in the catalogue.

  1. 01

    Concerts & festivals

    CO₂ em drops, confetti no encore, hazer constante. Sincronização à timecode ou ao cue de régie.

  2. 02

    Weddings

    Sparklers à entrada, fumo pesado na primeira dança, confetti no sim. Discreto, indoor-safe, sem cheiro a queimado.

  3. 03

    Launches & corporate

    Product reveal with CO₂ and jets, fog curtain for speaker entry, closing streamers.

  4. 04

    Private parties

    Compact operation, tailored to the room. Low-profile combinations that don't dominate the space.

  5. 05

    Photo & video shoots

    Hazer atmosférico, fumo controlado, confetti com slow-fall para captação de imagem.

  6. 06

    Mega-events

    Multi-cue setups, equipment redundancy, prior coordination with firefighters and venue security.

Safety & licensing

Risk analysis before the first effect.

Every operation is preceded by a technical visit and risk analysis of the space. When the type of effect or capacity requires it, prior coordination with firefighters, private venue security and event management.

  • Technical visit and written deployment plan.
  • Plano de contingência e cold zone definida.
  • Trained operators with active professional insurance.
  • Compliance with ANEPC regulations, health authorities and applicable municipal regulations.

How to request

Tell us the moment. We design the effect.

Indicate the event type, date, space and the moment when the effect enters your narrative (entry, drop, encore, first dance, reveal). We return a written proposal with type, scale, sync and associated licensing.

Frequently asked questions

How much do cold pyrotechnics cost for a show?

Price per machine and per moment. Includes licensed operator and venue-approved safety plan. For team entries, celebrations or specific moments, request with date and number of cues leads to a detailed quote.

Do you operate special effects in stadiums and arenas?

Yes. Experience in stadiums (José Gomes, Amadora) and arenas in Lisbon. Full protocol compliance with the sports entity, venue security and firefighters. Licensed in-house equipment.

Which effect, at which point in the night?

Tell us the context. We'll structure the right solution.
