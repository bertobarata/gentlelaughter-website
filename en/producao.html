Production assistance.

Stages, scenography, sound, light, video, generators. Design, workshop build, on-site rigging, show-day operation and tear-down — all by the same crew.

Four phases

From technical drawing to tear-down.

We work with producers, agencies and end clients. Continuity across phases is what keeps show day surprise-free.

01

Technical design

Creative brief, site plan, technical rider, equipment list and build schedule.

02

Build

Workshop scenography: panels, backdrops, modular structures, one-off pieces. Materials chosen for real stage use.

03

On-site rigging

Build-up, rigging, sound/light/video install, set dressing, soundcheck and final cue with the show caller.

04

Operation & tear-down

Crew on stage throughout, radio comms with the booth and overall coordination. Load-out, transport, reset.

Show day

Crew on stage, radio in the booth.

Our crew on stage throughout the operation, on radio with the booth and the overall coordinator. Continuous reporting, fast decisions, no noise in the chain of command.

  • Dedicated stage coordinator throughout the event.
  • Radio comms with earpieces; kit provided by us.
  • Cue sheet delivered in writing before soundcheck.
  • Crew in uniform, ID-carded, with active insurance.

How to book

Tell us the idea — we send back a plan.

Send date, location, event type, capacity and visual references (if any). We return a site plan, equipment list, schedule and written quote before touching any kit.
